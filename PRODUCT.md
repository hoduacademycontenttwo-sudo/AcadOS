# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two decision-makers visit this site: (1) School principals and directors managing K-12 academics — they need to modernize testing, grading, and content without disrupting teachers; (2) Coaching institute owners running JEE/NEET/Olympiad prep — they need faster test creation, OMR automation, CBT mocks, and admissions CRM. Both are busy, skeptical buyers who decide within 90 seconds whether to book a demo.

## Product Purpose

AcadOS is a white-labeled Academic Operating System that replaces 5+ disconnected tools schools and coaching institutes use today. It covers exam paper generation (TestMaker), OMR scanning via smartphone, computer-based mock tests (CBT), a digital content library (Learners Hub), and an ERP + CRM suite. Success = a visitor books a demo call within one site visit.

## Brand Personality

Institutional authority. Precise. Urgent without being pushy. Like a McKinsey report designed by a modern software company — confident, evidence-led, zero fluff.

## Anti-references

- Government/NCERT style: boring, text-heavy, bureaucratic, low contrast
- Generic Indian EdTech (Byju's, Toppr): colorful, student-facing, gamified, not B2B
- Startup-flashy: unproven feel, excessive gradients, buzzword-heavy copy

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell.** Every claim needs a visual — product screenshot, diagram, or statistic. No claim without proof.
2. **One idea per fold.** Each section has a single dominant statement. Supporting detail is secondary and compact.
3. **Authority through restraint.** Fewer words carry more weight. Cut until every word earns its place.
4. **Visual momentum.** The eye should always know where to go next. Clear hierarchy, clear CTA at each step.
5. **Institutional confidence.** The design should feel like the platform is already used by 100+ institutions, not like it's still finding its feet.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. All body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast. All headings ≥ 3:1. Reduced motion support for all animations. Readable without images.
